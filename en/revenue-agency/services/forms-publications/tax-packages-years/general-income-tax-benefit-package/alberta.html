The following forms and schedules can be used to help you complete your tax return.

Steps

  1. Download or view your return and provincial forms

    • Federal tax

      • Income Tax and Benefit Return
        This is the main form you need to complete your taxes
      • Optional:
        • Federal income tax and benefit information
          Find out what's new for 2025 and get help completing your federal return
        • Federal Worksheet
          Use this worksheet to calculate some of the amounts to report on your return

    • Alberta tax

      • Form AB428 – Alberta Tax and Credits
        Calculate the Alberta tax and credits to report on your return
        • If you received business income from outside Alberta

        If you resided in Alberta on December 31, 2025 (or the date you left Canada if you emigrated in 2025) and all or part of your 2025 business income was earned and can be allocated to a permanent establishment outside Alberta, use Form T2203, Provincial and Territorial Taxes for Multiple Jurisdictions instead of Form AB428.

      • Optional:
        • Alberta Tax Information
          Find out what's new for residents of Alberta for 2025 and get help completing your Alberta tax and credits form
        • Worksheet AB428 – Alberta
          Calculate some of the amounts to report on your Alberta tax and credits form

  2. Choose other forms and schedules that may apply

    • Federal

      • Schedule 2 – Federal Amounts Transferred from your Spouse or Common-Law Partner
      • Schedule 3 – Capital Gains (or Losses)
      • Schedule 5 – Amounts for Spouse or Common-Law Partner and Dependants
      • Schedule 6 – Canada Workers Benefit
      • Schedule 7 – RRSP, PRPP and SPP Unused Contributions, Transfers, and HBP or LLP Activities
      • Schedule 8 – Canada Pension Plan Contributions and Overpayment
      • Schedule 9 – Donations and Gifts
      • Schedule 11 – Federal Tuition Amount and Canada Training Credit
      • Schedule 12 – Multigenerational Home Renovation Tax Credit
      • Schedule 13 – Employment Insurance Premiums on Self-Employment and Other Eligible Earnings
      • Schedule 15 – FHSA Contributions, Transfers and Activities

    • Alberta

      • Schedule AB(S2) – Provincial Amounts Transferred From Your Spouse or Common-Law Partner
      • Schedule AB(S11) – Alberta Tuition and Education Amounts
